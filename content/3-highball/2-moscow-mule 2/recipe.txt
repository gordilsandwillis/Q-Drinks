Method: 

- 
  step: >
    Mix salt and finely chopped jalapeno in
    a small bowl and spread out on a small
    plate
  _fieldset: recipeStepBlock
- 
  step: >
    Rub the rim of a highball glass with the
    lime wedge, the dip into a salt mixture,
    pushing through to evenly coat rim
  _fieldset: recipeStepBlock
- 
  step: >
    Fill the highball glass with ice and
    then pour in the tequila and squeeze in
    a lime
  _fieldset: recipeStepBlock
- 
  step: Pour in Q Grapefruit
  _fieldset: recipeStepBlock
- 
  step: >
    Gently Stir and garnish with a lime
    wedge
  _fieldset: recipeStepBlock

----

Servings: For one glass

----

Variations: Try it with your favorite mescal rather than tequila for a smokier drink.
