Title: Sparkling Greyhound

----

----

Recipemixer: grapefruit

----

Ingredients: 

- 
  ingredient: 1.5 oz Premium Vodka
  _fieldset: ingredient
- 
  ingredient: 5 oz Q Grapefruit
  _fieldset: ingredient
- 
  ingredient: Lime wedge
  _fieldset: ingredient
- 
  ingredient: Grapefruit peel
  _fieldset: ingredient

----

Servings: Makes one drink

----

Servingsnumber: 01

----

Method: Fill a highball glass with ice. Pour in the vodka and squeeze in the lime, leaving the lime wedge in the glass. Then pour in the Q Grapefruit, and garnish with the grapefruit peel and gently stir.

----

Variations: Rim the glass with salt for a Sparkling Salty Dog.
