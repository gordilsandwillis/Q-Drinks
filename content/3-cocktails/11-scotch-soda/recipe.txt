Title: Scotch and Soda

----

Image: scotch_soda-min.jpg

----

Recipemixer: club

----

Ingredients: 

- 
  ingredient: 2 oz. scotch
  _fieldset: ingredient
- 
  ingredient: 6 oz. Q Club Soda
  _fieldset: ingredient
- 
  ingredient: Lemon peel
  _fieldset: ingredient

----

Servings: Makes one drink

----

Servingsnumber: 01

----

Method: 

Fill a highball glass with ice.
Pour in the scotch and then pour in the Q Club Soda (from a fresh bottle).
Gently stir and garnish with a lemon peel.

----

Variations: Mix with any spirit of your choice for a fizzy, delicious cocktail.
