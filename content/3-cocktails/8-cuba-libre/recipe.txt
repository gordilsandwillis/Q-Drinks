Ingredients: 

- 
  ingredient: 2 oz. rum
  _fieldset: ingredient
- 
  ingredient: 6 oz. Q Kola
  _fieldset: ingredient
- 
  ingredient: Squeeze of lime
  _fieldset: ingredient
- 
  ingredient: Two lime wedges
  _fieldset: ingredient

----

Method: 

Fill a highball glass with ice.
Pour in the rum and squeeze in a lime.
Then pour in the Q Kola (from a fresh bottle).
Gently stir and garnish with a lime wedge.

----

Servings: Makes one drink

----

Variations: Try it with an overproof rum for a bigger kick.
