Title: Whiskey and Kola

----

Image: burbon_kola.jpg

----

Recipemixer: kola

----

Ingredients: 

- 
  ingredient: 1.5 oz Premium Bourbon
  _fieldset: ingredient
- 
  ingredient: 5 oz Q Kola
  _fieldset: ingredient
- 
  ingredient: Orange slice
  _fieldset: ingredient

----

Servings: Makes one drink

----

Servingsnumber: 01

----

Method: 

Fill a highball glass with ice. Pour in the bourbon and then add the Q Kola. Gently stir and garnish with an orange
slice.

----

Variations: Switch the bourbon for a rye for something with more punch. Lemon is also a good variation on the orange slice.
