Title: El Burro Flaco

----

Image: el_burro_flaco_1200.jpg

----

Recipemixer: light-ginger-beer

----

Ingredients: 

- 
  ingredient: 2 oz Reposado Tequila
  _fieldset: ingredient
- 
  ingredient: 5 oz Q Light Ginger Beer
  _fieldset: ingredient
- 
  ingredient: 2 Dashes orange bitters
  _fieldset: ingredient

----

Servings: Makes one drink

----

Servingsnumber: 01

----

Method: 

Fill a collins glass with ice. Pour in the tequila and squeeze in a lime wedge and add bitters. Then pour in the Q Ginger Beer. Gently stir and garnish with an orange slice and sage leaves.

Recipe from @the.Cocktail.bandit

----

Variations: Dehydrate citrus garnishes so they stay aromatic for longer.
