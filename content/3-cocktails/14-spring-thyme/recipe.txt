Ingredients: 

- 
  ingredient: 2 oz Premium London Dry Gin
  _fieldset: ingredient
- 
  ingredient: 5 oz Q Indian Tonic Water
  _fieldset: ingredient
- 
  ingredient: ½ oz Blood Orange Liqueur
  _fieldset: ingredient
- 
  ingredient: ½ oz Simple Syrup
  _fieldset: ingredient
- 
  ingredient: ¾ oz Fresh Lime Juice
  _fieldset: ingredient
- 
  ingredient: 2 Strawberries
  _fieldset: ingredient

----

Servings: Makes one drink

----

Servingsnumber: 01

----

Method: 

Muddle the strawberries in the bottom of a collins glass.  Fill glass with cubed ice and add all ingredients pouring the tonic last. Stir gently to mix the drink and garnish with strawberry slices and a thyme sprig.

Recipe from @apartment_bartender

----

Variations: Scale this recipe up to serve in a punch bowl for your guests to enjoy as a group by multiplying the recipe by 5.
