Title: London Dry Gin and Tonic

----

Image: indian_gin_tonic_2-min.jpg

----

Recipemixer: indian-tonic-water

----

Ingredients: 

- 
  ingredient: 2 oz. London Dry Gin
  _fieldset: ingredient
- 
  ingredient: 6 oz. Indian Tonic Water
  _fieldset: ingredient
- 
  ingredient: Two lime wedges
  _fieldset: ingredient

----

Servings: Makes one drink

----

Servingsnumber: 01

----

Method: 

Using a highball glass or a Spanish-style balloon glass, add the ice, then pour over your favorite London Dry gin.
Squeeze in a lime wedge.
Then pour in the Q Indian Tonic Water (from a fresh bottle).
Gently stir and garnish with a lime wedge.
Smell.
Sip.
And enjoy.

----

Variations: Have some fun. Swap in a different garnish, maybe a grapefruit, cucumber, or lime twist. Or muddle in a fresh herb, thyme can be great. Or even add an ounce of liqueur, something like pamplemousse or an elderflower.
