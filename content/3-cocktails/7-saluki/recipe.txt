Title: Saluki

----

Image: saluki-min.jpg

----

Recipemixer: grapefruit

----

Ingredients: 

- 
  ingredient: 2 oz. good vodka
  _fieldset: ingredient
- 
  ingredient: 6 oz. Q Grapefruit
  _fieldset: ingredient
- 
  ingredient: Two lime wedges
  _fieldset: ingredient

----

Servings: Makes one drink

----

Servingsnumber: 01

----

Method: 

Fill a highball glass with ice. 
Pour in the vodka and squeeze in the lime, leaving the lime wedge in the glass. 
Then pour in the Q Grapefruit (from a fresh bottle), garnish with the lime wedge and gently stir.

----

Variations: Rim the glass with salt for a Sparkling Salty Dog.
