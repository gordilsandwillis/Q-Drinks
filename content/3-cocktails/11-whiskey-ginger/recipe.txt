Title: Whiskey Ginger

----

Image: whiskey_ginger-min.jpg

----

Recipemixer: ginger-ale

----

Ingredients: 

- 
  ingredient: 1.5 oz Premium Whiskey
  _fieldset: ingredient
- 
  ingredient: 5 oz Q Ginger Ale
  _fieldset: ingredient
- 
  ingredient: Lemon peel
  _fieldset: ingredient

----

Servings: Makes one drink

----

Servingsnumber: 01

----

Method: Fill a highball glass with ice. Pour in the whiskey and then pour in the Q Ginger Ale. Gently stir and garnish with a lemon peel.

----

Variations: Try with a rye or a scotch for a crisp, refreshing drink.  A couple of dashes of bitters can add another layer of complexity to the drink.
