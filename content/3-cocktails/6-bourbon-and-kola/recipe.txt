Title: Bourbon and Kola

----

----

Recipemixer: kola

----

Ingredients: 

- 
  ingredient: 2 oz. bourbon
  _fieldset: ingredient
- 
  ingredient: 6 oz. Q Kola
  _fieldset: ingredient
- 
  ingredient: Orange slice
  _fieldset: ingredient

----

Servings: Makes one drink

----

Servingsnumber: 01

----

Method: 

Fill a highball glass with ice.
Pour in the bourbon and then add the Q Kola (from a fresh bottle).
Gently stir and garnish with an orange slice.

----

Variations: Try with a rye for something a bit less sweet.
