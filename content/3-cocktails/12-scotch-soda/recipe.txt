Title: Scotch and Soda

Recipemixer: club

----

Ingredients: 

- 
  ingredient: 1.5 oz Premium Scotch
  _fieldset: ingredient
- 
  ingredient: 5 oz Q Club Soda
  _fieldset: ingredient
- 
  ingredient: Lemon peel
  _fieldset: ingredient

----

Servings: Makes one drink

----

Servingsnumber: 01

----

Method: Fill a highball glass with ice. Pour in the scotch and then pour in the Q Club Soda. Gently stir and garnish with a lemon peel.

----

Variations: Add a pinch of salt to highlight the peat in an Islay whisky.
