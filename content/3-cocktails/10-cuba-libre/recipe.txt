-

  _fieldset: ingredient

----

Method: Fill a highball glass with ice. Pour in the rum and squeeze in a lime. Then pour in the Q Kola. Gently stir and garnish with the other lime wedge.

----

Servings: Makes one drink

----

Variations: Try it with an overproof rum for a bigger kick.
